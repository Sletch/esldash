Teaching Methods

Sentence Construction

  • S.V: Subject + Verb (foundation of a sentence)
  • S.V.A: Matching Subject and Verb (agree in number and tense)
  • Obj(n.d)+ P: Object + Preposition (complete the action with context)
  • */Adj/Adv*: Adjectives and Adverbs (enrich detail and description)
  • C1, conj. C2: Linking Clauses (build complexity and flow)

Paragraph Framework

4-Step Method:

  1. Read Text: Consume English text to understand the content.
  2. Identify Main Ideas: Use the 5 W's (Who, What, Where, When, Why) and 1 H (How).
  3. Create: Write a 4-sentence summary based on the text.
  4. Seek Feedback: Share your summary for improvement.
